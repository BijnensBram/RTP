# Fitting

Fitting function:

$f(\varepsilon) = B_1 e ^{- \frac{\varepsilon a B_2 L}{c^2}}$

* $L$ = length of cell
* $a$ = fliprate
* $c$ = propulsion speed
* $\varepsilon$ = field
